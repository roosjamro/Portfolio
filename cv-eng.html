About Me

My name is Roos Jamro, I am 19 years old and currently pursuing an associate degree in Programming at PXL in Hasselt.
During my high school years, I explored various study fields, including Latin, Modern Sciences, Industrial Sciences, and Social-Technical Sciences.
While studying Industrial Sciences, I was introduced to Arduino. I found it so fascinating that I quickly realized I wanted to delve deeper into it in the future.

After graduating high school, I chose to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Electronics-ICT at UCLL in Diepenbeek.
I soon discovered that this program didn’t entirely align with my interests, particularly due to its focus on electronics.
After reviewing my options, I decided to switch to the associate degree in Programming, a choice that suits me better.
